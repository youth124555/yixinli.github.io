ABOUT / 关于我

I am a Ph.D. student in International Relations at Renmin University of China. My research interests include Indo-Pacific security, alliance theory, Political Psychology and great-power strategic behavior.

My current research examines how states adjust their strategic preferences and external behavior under changing power structures, regional security pressures, and perceptions of long-term strategic trends. I am particularly interested in U.S. alliance and partner networks in the Indo-Pacific, South China Sea security dynamics, and the interaction between great-power competition and regional order.

我目前是一名中国人民大学国际关系专业的博士生。我的研究兴趣包括印太安全、联盟理论、政治心理学以及大国战略行为。

我目前关注国家在权力结构变化、地区安全压力和长期战略趋势认知影响下如何调整战略偏好与对外行为，尤其关注美国在印太地区的盟伴网络、南海安全动态，以及大国竞争与地区秩序之间的互动关系。
