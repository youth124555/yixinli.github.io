About My Research

My research interests include Indo-Pacific security, alliance theory, Political Psychology and great-power strategic behavior.

My current research examines how states adjust their strategic preferences and external behavior under changing power structures, regional security pressures, and perceptions of long-term strategic trends.

Research Areas

  • National strategy and strategic behavior
  • Indo-Pacific security architecture
  • Alliance politics and minilateral cooperation
  • U.S. alliance and partner networks
  • Political Psychology
  • Great-power competition and regional order
