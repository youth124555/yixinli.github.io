## About Me
I am currently a PhD student in International Relations at the Renmin University of China (RUC). My research interests include national strategy, Political Psychology, Indo-Pacific security, alliance theory and maritime disputes.

## Know More About Me

Beyond academic research, I have some experience in journalism, public affairs, and cross-border business. I previously worked as a financial journalism intern, where I covered topics such as retail supply chains, platform competition, and Chinese firms’ overseas strategy. These experiences have shaped my interest in the intersection of strategy, political economy, and regional affairs.

I am also interested in academic writing, international communication, and data-assisted research methods. I am currently exploring how Python-based data collection and text analysis can be used to study diplomatic interactions and maritime security dynamics.
